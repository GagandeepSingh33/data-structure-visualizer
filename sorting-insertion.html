What is Insertion Sort?

Insertion Sort treats the left part of the array as sorted and picks one element at a time from the unsorted part, inserting it into the correct position within the sorted part.

It works similarly to sorting playing cards in your hand: you take the next card and shift larger cards to the right until you find the right place to insert it.

Complexity & Legend

Time complexity: best case O(n) for an already sorted array, average and worst cases O(n²) due to shifting; space complexity: O(1), and the algorithm is stable.
