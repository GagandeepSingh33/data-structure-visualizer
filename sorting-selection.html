What is Selection Sort?

Selection Sort is a comparison-based algorithm that divides the array into a sorted and an unsorted part, then repeatedly selects the minimum element from the unsorted part and swaps it with the first unsorted position.

After each pass, the sorted region grows from the left while the unsorted region shrinks, until all elements are placed in their correct positions.

Complexity & Legend

Time complexity: best, average, and worst cases are all O(n^2), because it always scans the remaining unsorted elements; space complexity: O(1) since it sorts in-place.
