What is Shell Sort?

Shell Sort is an optimization of insertion sort that starts by comparing elements far apart using a gap, gradually reducing the gap until it becomes 1.

By first sorting elements that are far apart, Shell Sort reduces the amount of shifting needed in the final insertion-sort-like pass, improving performance on many inputs.

Complexity & Legend

Time complexity depends on the gap sequence; with a simple n/2, n/4, ..., 1 sequence, the worst case is around O(n²), while practical performance is often better than insertion sort. Space complexity is O(1), and the algorithm is generally not stable.
